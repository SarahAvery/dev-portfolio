About Me

Hi there, my name is Sarah Avery. I am a self-taught junior front-end web developer, through completing Codecademy and other online courses and tutorials. My skills primarily consist of HTML, CSS/SCSS, and some Javascript. Through Codecademy I have completed their introductory courses for JS and React. On my own, I have designed and developed several websites just to learn and have fun.
